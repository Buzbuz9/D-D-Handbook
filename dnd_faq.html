Frequently Asked Question

How do I resolve issues with the people I am playing with?
There is no correct answer, rember that they are people too and most issues can be talked out.
How many players should there be in a game?
4-6 is the recommened player amount, but you can play with more or fewer people.
Can [Insert Race] Become A [Insert Class]?
Yes. There are no racial restrictions on classes. A half-orc or a lizardfolk can become a wizard, an elf or a halfling can be a barbarian, etc.
Does a [Insert Class] have to be [Insert Alignment]?
No, in 5e there are no class alignment restrictions.
How Do I Multiclass?
1st your DM has to allow it, but Multiclassing allows a character to gain a level in a different class, such as a fighter taking a level of a rogue. You can do this when you accumulate enough experience points to level up.
Is there a lot of math involved?
Just basic addition and maybe a little basic multiplication.
How long does a game take to play?
As long as you want. As a group decide on how much time you want to play the game for. Often players will meet up regularly to play with the same characters on different adventures like a television show, but you can easily play for an hour or so in a single evening and enjoy a short, thrilling adventure.
This seems like a lot of work. Is there a faster easier way to start?
Yes, you can use a premade character instead of making your own and start playing that way.
